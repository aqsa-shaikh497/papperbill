Frequently Asked Questions

Is Paperbill really free to use? +

Yes, Paperbill offers a completely free version with all essential features for small retailers including unlimited invoicing, basic inventory management, and expense tracking. There's also a premium version with additional capabilities like multi-user access, advanced reporting, and priority support for growing businesses.

Can I use Paperbill without internet? +

Absolutely! Paperbill works completely offline, and your data syncs automatically when you're back online. This makes it perfect for areas with unreliable internet connections. All features including invoicing, inventory management, and reporting are available offline.

How does Paperbill help with GST compliance? +

Paperbill automatically generates GST-compliant invoices with all required fields including HSN/SAC codes, GSTIN, and tax breakdowns. It prepares GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B reports that can be directly uploaded to the GST portal. The software also tracks input tax credit and calculates your tax liability automatically.

Can I access my data on multiple devices? +

Yes, Paperbill allows you to access your business data across multiple devices. Your data syncs securely between your phone, tablet, and computer. With premium plans, you can have multiple users accessing the same business data simultaneously from different devices.

What is restaurant billing software +

Restaurant billing software is a digital tool designed to streamline the billing process in restaurants. It helps restaurant owners and managers keep track of orders, generate bills, process payments, and maintain records of sales and transactions.

Can PaperBill's food court billing software handle multiple outlets in a food court? +

Yes, PaperBill's food court billing software is perfect for managing multiple outlets in a food court. The software helps you streamline billing for each counter, track sales separately, and generate comprehensive reports for better performance management. It ensures efficient coordination and smooth operations across the outlets.

Does PaperBill's cafe billing software support mobile billing? +

Yes, PaperBill's cafe billing software supports mobile billing, allowing cafe owners to process orders and payments directly from a mobile device. This flexibility is particularly useful in small cafes where space and time efficiency are crucial.
